Discover Ethiopia's Ancient Wonders

At Overland Ethiopia Tours, we unveil a country of brilliant beauty, mystery, and extraordinary natural phenomena. Ethiopia stands proudly in the Horn of Africa as a land of great antiquity, with culture and traditions stretching back 3000 years. Home to Africa's most ancient kingdom and one of the world's first monarchies, Ethiopia boasts a unique alphabet over 2000 years old and remains the only African nation to maintain independence from European colonization.
